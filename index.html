Privacy Policy for KT HOME AI

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

Introduction

Altimedia is committed to protecting the privacy of users of our internal Android application ("KT HOME AI"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect voice audio files and related data when you use the App. The App is designed for internal use by candidates hired by Altimedia to record specific sentences provided by us. It is not publicly available.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Our app collects personal information (phone number) to create user accounts for candidate onboarding and voice recordings to verify candidate identity. This data is securely transmitted to our server via HTTPS and stored for internal use. We do not share this data with third parties

1.2 Voice Audio Files

The App’s core function is to record audio files of your voice as you speak sentences provided by us. These audio files are collected when you use the App’s recording feature.

1.3 Permissions

The App requires the WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_AUDIO permission to:

  • Read the audio files for processing within the App.

1.4 Other Data

We may collect limited metadata related to the audio files, such as file names, timestamps and processing of recordings.

2. How We Use Your Information

The voice audio files and related data are used solely for the following purposes:

  • Internal Evaluation: To evaluate the recordings as part of the candidate selection or training process.
  • Storage and Management: To store and organize audio files securely for internal use.
  • Quality Assurance: To ensure the App functions correctly and recordings meet our requirements.

We do not use your audio files for any purpose beyond the internal objectives of Altimedia.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Audio files are stored in a designated folder on your device, accessible only to the App for processing. Upon completion of the evaluation process, audio files may be transferred to our secure internal servers for further review or archiving.
  • Security: We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your audio files from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to audio files is restricted to authorized personnel within Altimedia who require access for the purposes outlined above.
  • Retention: Audio files will deleted and application also unintsall from user devcie when we accepted audio files..

4. Sharing of Information

The App is an internal tool, and your audio files are not shared with third parties outside Altimedia, except as required by law or with your explicit consent. Within Altimedia, access is limited to employees or contractors directly involved in the candidate evaluation or App maintenance processes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

As an internal application, your use of the App is part of your engagement with [Your Company Name]. You may:

  • Request access to the audio files you have recorded.
  • Request deletion of your audio files, subject to our internal retention policies or legal obligations.
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about your data (see Section 7).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. You will be notified of any material changes through the App or by other means within Altimedia.
